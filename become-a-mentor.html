Mentor Guidelines

As a mentor, your only responsibility is that respond to any mentee who contacts you by guiding and advising them to the best of your ability. For any issues, clarifications, please email the admin at hello@mentorabroad.com

Mentor Info

To volunteer to become a mentor, please fill out the form on the left. You must be currently enrolled in a college/university in a different country from your home country. Your will be required to provide your institution email (youremail@college.edu)as a confirmation of this. For any questions, please email us at hello@mentorabroad.com and we will respond as soon as possible.
